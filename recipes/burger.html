Burger

Description

Europeans love burger dishes and it is one of the oldest dishes.

Ingredients

  • Bun
  • Salt
  • Cheese
  • Masala
  • Chicken
  • Vegetable

Steps

  1. Cut the bun into two pieces.
  2. Put All the stuffs in between the bun.
  3. After keep the bun in a oven for 10 minutes and serve it with yummy.
